# AI/ML Training Frameworks

## Major Frameworks

**PyTorch**: Research standard (dynamic graphs, 60% market share, excellent ecosystem)
**TensorFlow**: Production focus (static graphs, TF Serving, declining but stable)
**JAX**: Google's framework (functional, XLA compilation, high performance)
**Lightning**: PyTorch wrapper (removes boilerplate, research velocity)

**Recommendation**: PyTorch for research and production, JAX for maximum performance.
