# WebAssembly & Serverless Evolution

## WebAssembly Trends

**Server-side WASM**: WasmEdge, Wasmtime, Spin (Fermyon)
**Benefits**: Polyglot serverless, <100ms cold starts, better isolation
**Timeline**: 30-40% adoption by 2027 for edge computing

## Serverless 2.0

**Improvements**: Sub-100ms cold starts, stateful serverless, better debugging
**Key Technologies**: Firecracker microVMs, WASM-based runtimes, durable execution
