# Data Engineering Decision Matrix

## Quick Selection

**Large-scale batch**: Apache Spark
**Fast analytics**: DuckDB or Polars
**Event streaming**: Kafka (established) or Redpanda (performance)
**Complex workflows**: Dagster (data pipelines) or Temporal (microservices)
**Simple ETL**: Airflow
